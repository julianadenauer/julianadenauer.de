Julian Adenauer is an technologist and creative engineer based in Berlin. He is interested in the intersection of art, design, technology and culture. He examines emerging technologies and their impact on society and creative expression.

Since 2009 he's one half of the artist/inventor duo Sonice Development. In 2012 he co-founded retune. – a platform at the intersection of art and technology with an annual festival at its core.
He works as a freelancer in a variety of constellations: as realizer of artworks, organizer of events, workshop leader and consultant.
